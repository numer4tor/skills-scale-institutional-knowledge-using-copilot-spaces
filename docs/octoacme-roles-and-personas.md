# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Design/UX Lead

### Role Summary
Design/UX Leads ensure products are user-centered, accessible, and intuitive. They collaborate with Product and Engineering to translate requirements into actionable design specifications and validate usability.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design systems
- Define accessibility and usability standards
- Collaborate on feature specifications with Product and Engineering
- Review designs for consistency and quality
- Advocate for user needs throughout the project lifecycle

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce rework through early-stage design validation
- Build a cohesive, scalable design system

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and feedback sessions
- User research findings and recommendations
- Weekly sync with Product and Engineering leads
- Usability test reports and metrics

---

## QA/Testing Lead

### Role Summary
QA/Testing Leads define and execute quality strategies to ensure features meet acceptance criteria and perform reliably. They own test planning, automation, and quality gate approvals.

### Responsibilities
- Define test strategy and approach for each initiative
- Create and maintain automated and manual test cases
- Execute acceptance and regression testing
- Track and prioritize defect fixes
- Validate release readiness before production deployment
- Collaborate on Definition of Done and acceptance criteria
- Drive test automation and tooling improvements

### Goals
- Ensure high-quality, reliable releases
- Reduce escaped defects and production incidents
- Accelerate testing cycles through automation

### Typical Communication
- Test plan reviews with Engineering and Product
- Defect reports and triage discussions
- Release readiness assessments
- Quality metrics and test coverage reports

---

## DevOps/Platform Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps/Platform Engineers maintain infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, and deployment automation. They enable fast, reliable releases and operational excellence.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines and infrastructure
- Automate deployment, testing, and rollback processes
- Monitor system health, performance, and security
- Provide runbooks and incident response procedures
- Collaborate on scaling and reliability requirements
- Support teams with tooling and troubleshooting

### Goals
- Enable fast, automated, reliable deployments
- Minimize deployment risk and downtime
- Maintain system observability and performance

### Typical Communication
- Infrastructure and deployment architecture discussions
- Incident response coordination
- Release readiness checks and deployment coordination
- Performance and reliability metrics

---

## Scrum Master / Agile Coach

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters and Agile Coaches facilitate team processes, remove blockers, and help teams improve their velocity and collaboration. They enable the team to self-organize effectively.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate standups, planning, review, and retrospective meetings
- Identify and help resolve team blockers
- Track sprint velocity and health metrics
- Coach the team on agile practices and continuous improvement
- Remove impediments that block team productivity
- Guide adoption of Agile/Scrum principles

### Goals
- Enable sustainable team velocity and morale
- Improve process efficiency and decision-making
- Foster psychological safety and continuous learning

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint ceremonies
- Retrospective action item tracking
- Velocity and burndown charts
- Coaching conversations and team feedback

---

## Security Officer

### Role Summary
Security Officers integrate security into the product and process. They ensure compliance, mitigate risks, and enable the team to build secure features.

### Responsibilities
- Review security requirements and acceptance criteria
- Conduct security threat assessments and code reviews
- Manage security testing and vulnerability scanning in CI
- Advise on compliance and data protection requirements
- Respond to and triage security incidents
- Provide security training and best practices guidance

### Goals
- Prevent security vulnerabilities and breaches
- Enable the team to shift-left on security
- Maintain compliance and reduce security risk

### Typical Communication
- Security threat assessments and design reviews
- Vulnerability reports and remediation tracking
- Incident response and forensics
- Security training and policy updates
